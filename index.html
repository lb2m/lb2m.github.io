Welcome to LB2M

The Laboratory of Biotechnology and Bioinformatics of Microorganisms (LB2M) is composed of microbiologists with strong academic training and deep understanding of the molecular mechanisms underlying microbial evolution and adaptation. This expertise is integrated into academic and innovation-driven projects focused on microbial bioprospecting and biotechnology.

Our research topics include the systematics, evolution, and ecology of bacteria from Brazilian biomes. We also investigate how bacteria, fungi, and protists interact in soils, and how these interactions modify soil physicochemical properties and influence plant performance. We collaborate with institutions from Brazil, South America, and other regions worldwide.
